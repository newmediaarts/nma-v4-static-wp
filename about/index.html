Overview

What Makes NMA Different?

Rigorous Curriculum

The NMA curriculum is well-designed & comprehensive, offering a top-quality education comparable to top mainland art schools. Led by four full time faculty that are actively engaged in improving the curriculum.

View the Curriculum

Successful Students

The NMA curriculum is portfolio/demo-reel based to prepare students with real-world skills using the latest technologies. NMA students win awards locally and nationally and get hired by top companies.

Read About NMA Graduates

Established Program

Since 2001, NMA faculty have been iteratively improving the degree (for close to two decades). Local companies look to NMA for top quality graduates to hire every year. The forward-thinking NMA faculty @KapCC have big plans for the future.

Honors & Accolades

Award Winning Students

National & International Awards

NMA students have won NINE National Addy Awards! We are typically the only community college listed amongst the top design schools in the country for winning national student awards, sharing the podium with students from schools like like SVA, Parsons, & RISD.

NMA student practicum animated short films have won awards and have been screened at film festivals across the world,  including locally as part of CAFF at the Doris Duke Theater & Honolulu Museum of Art.

Local Awards
